Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

Overview

SwifKit does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal information that you directly enter into the app.

Advertising and Google AdMob

SwifKit uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. In connection with providing and measuring ads, Google and its partners may collect, use, and process information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information, including device type and operating system
  • Diagnostics, including crash and performance information
  • Ad interaction and other advertising-related data

Google processes this information under its own policies. For more information, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

Children’s privacy

SwifKit is intended for developers and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
